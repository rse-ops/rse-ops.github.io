What do we care about?

Changing HPC architectures and increasing complexity in simulations have caused major challenges to our software approaches at the lab. As we increasingly recognize the importance of collaboration and open source software, RADIUSS (Rapid Application Development via an Institutional Universal Software Stack) aims establish resources and practices to support lower cost and higher agility of development teams. The project includes:

  • Build automation and simplification
  • Computer science infrastructure
  • Math and physics libraries
  • Performance/workflow optimization
  • Data management and visualization
  • Coding policies
  • Standard deployment models
  • Documentation
